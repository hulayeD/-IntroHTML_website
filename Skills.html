I could teach someone how to craddle a lacrosse stick. All they need is a stick and a ball. After they place the ball into the pocket they can follow the steps below.

Steps

  1. Place your dominant hand halfway on your stick and place your non dominant a little above the bottom
  2. Bend your dominant hand and like a door hinge flick your wrist while holding your stick titled towards your dominant shoulder
  3. Keep the head near yours and increase your speed

Once you've done all these steps you can now craddle a lacrosse stick. Once you've gotten these steops down try it with the opposite hand or craddle even faster.If you still need help watch this video Craddling Tutorial
